FAQ
Questions, answered.
Is Petri vote buying? +
No. The payout gate checks only that you voted, never which way. Rewards are completely uncorrelated with vote direction.
What chain does it run on? +
The contract deploys on Osmosis testnet, but every market is a real Cosmos Hub governance proposal and the gate verifies a real Hub vote.
What is a mutation? +
A rare specimen minted to anyone who correctly predicts the unlikely outcome. It rewards contrarians and keeps the markets balanced.
How are odds calculated? +
Parimutuel. Your share of the winning pool, with no oracle and no order book. The odds are simply where the money has flowed.
